Begin the journey

Not a resume

A life shaped by movement.

Some lives are shaped by stillness. Mine has been shaped by movement.

My life has always been in motion — shaped by roads, cities, borders, and the unexpected turns that quietly change us.

I work in the world of movement, connecting places across China and the world. But I am equally drawn to slower things: Mediterranean light, old architecture, art, literature, photographs, and the feeling of discovering world cities.

As life enters a new chapter, I find myself collecting moments more carefully. This is a personal space for the places I have loved, the work that has shaped me, the images I want to remember, and the small beginnings still waiting to unfold.

My Journey

Career as a route map

A career shaped by movement — from air routes and ocean passages to the roads connecting China and Southeast Asia.

01

Air Freight

Tracing Invisible Lines

My journey began in air freight, where cities are connected by routes that cannot be seen. It was an early lesson in timing, precision, and the quiet coordination behind every departure.

02

Ocean Freight

Learning the Rhythm of Distance

Then came the sea — ports, containers, long passages, and a different sense of time. Ocean freight taught me that movement is not always about speed, but also about patience, planning, and perspective.

03

Cross-Border Road

Connecting Roads Across Borders

Today, my work moves closer to the ground, along the roads linking China and Southeast Asia. Border crossings, warehouses, trucks, and changing conditions become part of a living network — one that requires practical thinking, adaptability, and a clear sense of direction.

04

The Next Chapter

Moving Forward

Logistics has taught me to find a way through complexity: to connect places, solve problems, and keep moving when the route is not yet clear. I carry that perspective into new ideas, new tools, and every chapter still waiting to unfold.
